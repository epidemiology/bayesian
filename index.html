Resources

General Bayesian background

  • Bayesian Data Analysis by Gelman et al. (textbook)

    This is a general Bayesian data analysis textbook. It's not specific to epidemiology, but it's widely cited in the literature.

Epidemiology-specific resources

  • Chapter 24 in Modern Epidemiology by Rothman et al. (textbook)

    This chapter on Bayesian statistics for epidemiology contains much of the same content as the Greenland articles (see below).

  • Chapter 24 in Methods in Epidemiological Research by Dohoo et al. (textbook)

    This chapter in MER talks mostly about advanced methods, but it has a good summary of the difference between Bayesian and Frequentist approaches at the beginning, as well as a good description of MCMC models.

  • Bayesian perspectives for epidemiological research by Greenland (series of journal articles)

    :
    1. Foundations and basic methods
    2. Regression analysis
    3. Bias analysis via missing-data methods
